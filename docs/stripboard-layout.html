Track Cuts Needed

  • Row 4, between C–D: isolate SN65:GND from ESP32:GP4
  • Rows 1–8, col I: (×8) isolate ESP32 right pins from left traces
  • Row 7, col C: isolate CANH from GP7
  • Row 8, col C: isolate CANL from GP8
  • Row 11, between E–F: isolate D cathode from D anode

Voltage Divider (rows 2→4→9)

Sensor 5V → R10K → GP4 (3.33V) → R20K → GND

  • R20K: legs at E2 (GND row) and E4 (GP4 row)
  • R10K: legs at F4 (GP4 row) and F9 (signal row)

Vout = 5V × 20K/(10K+20K) = 3.33V ✓

Power Protection (rows 10–11)

  • D1 (1N4007): Anode at I11, cathode at F11. Band → LEFT.
  • C1 (100µF 50V): + at D11, − at D10. Observe polarity!
  • 12V in: J10   GND in: J10 row 10

Connectors

  • J10 (12V): 12V power input at col J row 11
  • J10 (GND): GND power input at col J row 10
  • J9 (DATA): Sensor signal at col J row 9
  • CAN-H: col A row 7
